🎵 About Music Genre Classifier

This web application uses deep learning to automatically predict the genre of music from audio files. Upload any song and get instant predictions powered by a convolutional neural network (CNN).

How It Works

The classifier follows these steps:

  • Audio Processing: Your audio file is divided into 30-second segments
  • Spectrogram Generation: Each segment is converted to a mel-spectrogram (a visual representation of sound)
  • CNN Prediction: A trained neural network analyses each spectrogram
  • Ensemble Averaging: Predictions from all segments are averaged for the final result

Supported Genres

The model can classify music into 10 genres:

  • Blues
  • Classical
  • Country
  • Disco
  • Hip-hop
  • Jazz
  • Metal
  • Pop
  • Reggae
  • Rock

Training Data

The model was trained on the GTZAN Genre Collection dataset, which contains 1,000 audio tracks (100 per genre), each 30 seconds long. This is a widely-used benchmark dataset in music information retrieval research.

Technical Details

  • Model Architecture: Convolutional Neural Network (CNN) with 3 convolutional layers
  • Framework: PyTorch
  • Audio Processing: Librosa
  • Web Framework: Flask
  • Input Format: MP3, WAV, FLAC, OGG (maximum 20 megabytes)

Limitations

Please note:

  • Predictions are based on patterns learned from the training data and may not always be accurate. The model achieved 62.63% accuracy on the validation dataset.
  • Subgenres or fusion genres may not be classified accurately
  • Audio quality may affect prediction accuracy

Privacy

Your uploaded audio files are processed in-memory and are not permanently stored on the server. Files are temporarily cached for playback and automatically deleted after 30 minutes.
